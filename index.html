About This Database

The TeamAg Organic Transition Materials Review Database is a compliance reference tool for organic producers, handlers, and certifiers. It provides a searchable index of materials evaluated against the USDA National Organic Program (NOP) National List (7 CFR §205.601–606).

How to Use

  • Search by material name, description, or CFR reference
  • Filter by category (Crops, Livestock, Processing), compliance status, use type, and substance type
  • Click any row to view full details including annotations, restrictions, and regulatory references
  • Export filtered results to CSV for your records

Compliance Status Key

Allowed Material is permitted for use in organic production under the listed conditions.
Restricted Material is allowed only with specific restrictions or annotations.
Prohibited Material is prohibited for use in organic production.

Disclaimer

This database is for reference purposes only. Always verify compliance with your certifying agent before using any material. The National List is subject to updates by the National Organic Standards Board (NOSB) and USDA. Last updated: March 2026.
